YouTube Egg Cake Recipe

YouTube Egg Cake

Look at this easy to make, popular looking recipe that was suggested to me via YouTube! The chef speaks Chinese so this is pretty authentic.

I hope you like onions and not much flavor, because this recipe has a ton of BOTH!

Ingredients

  • 6 Egs
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 sweet onions
  • 1 tbl soy sauce

Steps

  1. Dice pepper, carrots, and 1 of 2 sweet onions
  2. Beat 4 eggs in seperate bowl
  3. Combine eggs, vegetables, salt, pepper, soy sauce, potato starch and flour until mixed
  4. Heat non-stick skillet with olive oil to medium heat
  5. Slice onion rings, and place onto skillet. Fill rings with egg mixture, topping with sesame seeds.
  6. Cover, cook 3 minutes. Flip egg cake, cover, and cook 3 more minutes
