Frozen Pizza Recipe

Frozen Pizza

Tired after a long day at work? Looking for that slightly less-than-healthy dinner? Check out this amazing recipe for FROZEN PIZZA.

Ingredients

  • Pizza (frozen)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Remove pizza from plastic wrap
  3. Place directly on middle oven rack
  4. Bake for 12-14 minutes
  5. Let stand for 2 minutes (or seconds) before cutting and serving
