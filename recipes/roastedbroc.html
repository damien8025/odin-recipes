Roasted Broccoli Recipe

Roasted Brocooli

A quick an easy side dish for dinner! Chop up some fresh broccoli and coat with salt, pepper, and olive oil.

Roast in the oven at 450 degrees for about 20-25 minutes, and then BAM! You've got a delicious dish that should satisfy the entire family.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • EVOO
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees
  2. Cut up broccoli into bite sized pieces
  3. Toss broccoli with olive oil, salt, and pepper
  4. Spread evenly on parchment lined baking sheet
  5. Roast for 20-25 minutes (to taste)
